TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Graph Characteristics:
  • Nodes: 41,718
  • Edges: 64,126 (undirected)
  • Component: 1 (fully connected)
  • Diameter: ~295 segments
  • Source: OpenStreetMap Colombian road network

Algorithm Characteristics:
  • DFS: O(b^d) time, O(b·d) space, explores 32k-34k nodes
  • BFS: O(b^d) time, O(b^d) space, explores 22k-35k nodes
  • UCS: O(b^⌈C*/ε⌉) time, optimal cost, explores 23k nodes
  • A*: O(b^d) time with good heuristic, explores 23k-77k nodes
  • IDS: O(b^d) time, O(b·d) space, explores 9-34k nodes
  • Multi-TSP: Exponential, solved with MST heuristic

Heuristic Characteristics:
  • Straight-line: Haversine distance, admissible, consistent
  • MST-based: Lower bound on TSP, cached for efficiency
  • Coverage: Handles both single and multi-delivery problems
